Live, online, and interactive. Real-time learning, hands-on practice, immediate Q&A.

No. Workshop is designed for all backgrounds. If you can use web applications, you can master this.
What tools do I need?
Computer, internet, and access to AI platforms (ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini - free versions work).

Each batch is 2 intensive weekend days (usual: 5 hours/day, 2-7 PM IST). Flexible timing based on batch preference—we'll confirm yours.

We use MS Teams for easy video and screen-sharing (free app, no downloads needed). Link sent 24 hours prior. If issues arise, join our live chat support—your success comes first.

You'll build a custom AI Playbook, configure your personal AI companion, and deliver a real project (e.g., career kit or business tool). Walk away with prompt engineering mastery, 25+ tool integrations, and a certificate to showcase on LinkedIn.
